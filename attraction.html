Explore Boston's Best Attractions

Discover the most iconic places in and around Boston

Freedom Trail

Walk the 2.5-mile route that leads to 16 historically significant sites.

Fenway Park

Visit the oldest ballpark in Major League Baseball and home of the Red Sox.

Charles River

Enjoy scenic views and recreational activities along the Charles River.

Boston Common

Explore America's oldest public park in the heart of the city.

Museum of Fine Arts

Discover one of the largest and most comprehensive art museums in the world.

New England Aquarium

Home to thousands of aquatic animals and interactive exhibits for all ages.

Harvard University

Visit the iconic Ivy League campus, home to the oldest university in the U.S.

Salem, MA

Explore the infamous Witch Trials town, known for its rich history and museums.

Cape Cod

Relax on the scenic beaches and charming towns of Cape Cod, just outside Boston.
